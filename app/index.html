Lemon Colonies

A simple cooperative idle colony builder

Build, gather, and grow alongside other players in a shared world. Develop your colony, manage resources, and cooperate with the colonies around you to thrive.

Public Alpha — Gameplay is very barebones right now. Expect bugs, missing features, and occasional resets as we build things out.

Continue with Discord

We use Discord just to recognise you, the same way some sites let you "Sign in with Google". Lemon Colonies cannot read your messages, see which servers you're in, or post anything on your behalf. All the game receives is your username, avatar and a discord ID. Those will never be shared with anyone else. The game will assign you a random username on first start.

Not made for mobile

Lemon Colonies needs a keyboard and a bigger screen. Open this page on a desktop or laptop to play.
